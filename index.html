Streamlined Workflow

We interface seamlessly your Modalities to dicom reciever to push the images to our cloud platform to deliver reports in 4 simple steps.

01

Upload Scan

Technicians upload DICOM images securely to our encrypted cloud server through dicom interface directly.

02

Auto-Routing

Our system assigns the case to the most qualified specialist available (e.g., Neuro, MSK).

03

Expert Reporting

Certified radiologists analyze the images and dictate a detailed report using voice-recognition tools.

04

Instant Access

The final verified report is instantly available for download from the system.
